Job Title: AI Solutions Architect
Location: Remote or Hybrid (Preferred: US-based)
Department: AI Engineering / Solutions
Seniority Level: Senior-Level
________________


About the Role
We’re looking for a strategic and hands-on AI Solutions Architect who can bridge the gap between business needs and technical implementation. You will work with cross-functional teams to design, propose, and oversee the development of AI-powered solutions, focusing on real-world applications that create measurable business impact.
________________


Key Responsibilities
* Architect and design scalable AI systems that leverage LLMs, vector databases, and prompt engineering.
* Collaborate with stakeholders to translate business requirements into technical specs.
* Lead technical discovery calls and client-facing solution design sessions.
* Develop and maintain reusable architectures, templates, and reference implementations.
* Advise on model selection (e.g. OpenAI, Claude, Gemini), data strategy (e.g. RAG, embeddings), and deployment methods (e.g. self-hosted, serverless).
* Oversee integration with APIs, third-party tools, and workflow platforms like n8n.
* Stay current with AI trends, ensuring proposed solutions reflect best practices.
________________


Minimum Qualifications
* 3+ years of experience in AI/ML, automation, or backend development.
* Strong understanding of LLM ecosystems (e.g. OpenAI, Anthropic, Mistral, Cohere).
* Proficiency with tools such as n8n, LangChain, or custom agent frameworks.
* Experience with vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, or Qdrant) and retrieval techniques (RAG).
* Familiarity with prompt engineering, tool/function calling, and system design.
* Excellent communication and documentation skills.
________________


Preferred Qualifications
* Prior experience delivering AI solutions to clients or as part of an agency.
* Understanding of data privacy, security, and ethical considerations in AI.
* Experience with Python or JavaScript-based AI development workflows.
* Strong grasp of business workflows and how AI can enhance them.
* Previous exposure to low-code platforms or custom integrations.
________________


Success in This Role Looks Like:
* Delivered at least 2 end-to-end AI agent or automation projects.
* Created scalable solutions that were adopted and used successfully by teams or clients.
* Provided architecture that reduced delivery time or operational complexity.
